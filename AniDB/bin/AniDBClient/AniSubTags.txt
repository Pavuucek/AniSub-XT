Posible test:
A - Anime id or name; romaji, kanji and english, but not synonyms and short names
G - Group id, short name or 'unknown'
E - Episode name or episode number
Q - Quality [unknown, very high, high, med, low, very low, corrupted, eyecancer]
R - Rip source [unknown, camcorder, TV, DTV, VHS, VCD, SVCD, LD, DVD, HKDVD, www]
T - Anime type [unknown, TV, OVA, Movie, Other, web]
Y - Anime Year
D - Dub language
S - Sub language
P - Local file path
N - Genres
I - Comparing tag or tag is defined
H - Comparing tag(s) between another tag(s)
C - Video or Audio codec
8 - Is Anime 18+ restricted
W - Total episodes count
